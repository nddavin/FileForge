# FileForge Community Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age, body size, visible or invisible disability
- Ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status
- Nationality, personal appearance, race, religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## 2. Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

| Behavior | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Welcoming Language** | Using inclusive, friendly greetings |
| **Respectful Disagreement** | Debating ideas constructively |
| **Graceful Feedback** | Accepting and giving feedback graciously |
| **Acknowledgment** | Crediting others' contributions |
| **Focus on Solution** | Working together toward common goals |
| **Empathy** | Understanding others' perspectives |

### Unacceptable Behaviors

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

| Behavior | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Harassment | Unwelcome sexual attention, stalking |
| Discrimination | Racist, sexist, or exclusionary comments |
| Doxxing | Publishing private information without consent |
| Trolling** | Deliberately inflammatory contributions |
| Personal Attacks | Insults, derogatory remarks |
| Spam** | Unsolicited promotional content |
| Unauthorized Access | Attempting to access restricted systems |

## 3. Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Discord/Slack community channels
- Documentation and wikis
- Support forums and email lists
- In-person events and meetups
- Social media accounts

## 4. Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

Report violations to the **Code of Conduct Team**:

| Contact | Purpose | Response Time |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| conduct@fileforge-app.com | General concerns | 48 hours |
| security@fileforge-app.com | Security-related | 24 hours |
| [Anonymous Form](https://fileforge-app.com/report) | Anonymous reporting | 72 hours |

### Investigation Process

```
1. REPORT
   ↓
2. ACKNOWLEDGMENT (within 24 hours)
   ↓
3. INVESTIGATION (confidential)
   ↓
4. DETERMINATION (within 7 days)
   ↓
5. RESOLUTION (appropriate action)
   ↓
6. FOLLOW-UP (30 days)
```

### Confidentiality

All reports will be kept confidential. Information will only be shared with those who need to know for investigation and resolution.

## 5. Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these guidelines in determining consequences:

### 5.1 Correction

**Behavior**: Inappropriate language or unintentional disrespect

**Consequence**:
- Private written warning
- Clear explanation of why behavior was inappropriate
- No public acknowledgment required

### 5.2 Warning

**Behavior**: Single incident of inappropriate behavior

**Consequence**:
- Public written warning
- 30-day probation period
- Expected behavioral change
- Continued violations result in escalation

### 5.3 Temporary Ban

**Behavior**: Serious violation or repeated inappropriate behavior

**Consequence**:
- 7-day temporary ban from community
- No participation in any community space
- Private discussion of expectations

### 5.4 Permanent Ban

**Behavior**: Pattern of harassment or severe violation

**Consequence**:
- Permanent removal from community
- No return permitted
- May be reconsidered after 1 year (case-by-case)

## 6. Addressing Grievances

If you disagree with an enforcement decision:

1. **Request Review**: Email conduct@fileforge-app.com with explanation
2. **Review Process**: Senior leadership will review within 7 days
3. **Final Decision**: Binding resolution

Note: Temporary bans may be appealed after 30 days. Permanent bans may be appealed after 1 year.

## 7. Special Circumstances

### 7.1 Self-Correction

Individuals who self-correct inappropriate behavior before enforcement may receive reduced consequences.

### 7.2 Good Faith Reports

Reports made in good faith, even if later determined to be unfounded, will not result in retaliation.

### 7.3 Emergencies

Immediate threats to safety (violence, self-harm) will be escalated to appropriate authorities.
